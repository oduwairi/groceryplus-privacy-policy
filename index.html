Privacy Policy for GroceryPlus

Effective Date: January 12, 2025
Last Updated: January 12, 2025

Welcome to GroceryPlus ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application GroceryPlus (the "App").

By using GroceryPlus, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, Google profile photo, and optionally your phone number if provided by Google.
  • Shopping Lists: Items you add to shopping lists including product names, quantities, prices, descriptions, and categories.
  • Inventory Data: Items in your personal inventory including product details, quantities, custom categories, and organization preferences.
  • Recipe Information: Recipes you create or generate, including ingredients, instructions, and associated images.
  • Receipt Data: Information extracted from receipt photos you upload, including store names, purchase dates, item prices, and product details.
  • Voice Commands: Audio recordings temporarily processed for speech-to-text conversion when you use voice features.
  • Product Images: Photos you take or upload of products for your inventory or shopping lists.
  • Sharing Preferences: Email addresses of users you choose to share inventories or shopping lists with.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications, device type, and operating system version.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, frequency of use, AI service token consumption, and interaction patterns within the App.
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens to maintain your login session and authenticate API requests.
  • Timestamps: Creation and modification times for your data to enable synchronization and sorting.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Account: Basic profile information when you sign in with Google.
  • OpenFoodFacts: Product information retrieved when you scan barcodes, including nutritional data and product descriptions.
  • Location Data: Store locations you manually enter for price comparison features (we do not access your device's GPS location).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Authenticate your account and maintain secure sessions
  • Create, manage, and sync your shopping lists and inventories across devices
  • Generate AI-powered shopping suggestions and recipe recommendations
  • Process receipt images to extract purchase information
  • Convert voice commands to text for hands-free item entry
  • Enable sharing and collaboration features with other users
  • Send push notifications about shared list updates and invitations

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
  • Monitor AI token usage to manage service costs and prevent abuse
  • Debug technical issues and improve app performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 Communication

  • Send notifications about shared inventory or list changes
  • Alert you when someone accepts or declines your sharing invitations
  • Notify collaborators about shopping progress and checkout activities
  • Provide important service updates and security alerts

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly share inventories or shopping lists with other users
  • Information visible to users you collaborate with (your name, shared items, and updates)

3.2 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services that process information on our behalf:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, file storage, and push notifications
  • Google Cloud Vision API: Optical character recognition for receipt scanning
  • Google Gemini API: AI-powered receipt analysis and content generation
  • DeepSeek API: AI assistance for shopping list generation and item categorization
  • OpenFoodFacts API: Product information retrieval for barcode scanning

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, including:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Government agency requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To enforce our terms of service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is primarily stored in Google Firebase servers located in the United States
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access and performance
  • Image Storage: Product and receipt images are stored in Firebase Storage with secure access controls

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth 2.0
  • API keys protected with obfuscation techniques
  • User-based data isolation in Firebase Firestore
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability patches
  • Rate limiting on AI services to prevent abuse
Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View all information we have about you within the App
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information
  • Deletion: Delete individual items, lists, or your entire account
  • Portability: Export your shopping lists and inventory data
  • Withdrawal: Revoke sharing permissions at any time

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Manage push notification settings through your device settings
  • Control which notifications you receive within the App settings
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

5.3 Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Sign out from the App
  2. Contact us at the email address provided below
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days of verification

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Voice Recordings: Temporarily processed and immediately deleted after text conversion
  • Deleted Items: Removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Backup Data: May be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Children's Privacy

GroceryPlus is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information from our servers.
