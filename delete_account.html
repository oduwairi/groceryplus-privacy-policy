Account Deletion Request

⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

The following data will be permanently deleted:
  • Your user profile and personal information
  • All inventories and inventory items
  • All shopping lists and list items
  • All saved recipes
  • Your purchase history and checkout records
  • All shared access permissions (both given and received)
  • Notification preferences and history
  • Token usage data and limits
  • All uploaded images and documents
  • Your authentication credentials

Before You Delete Your Account

Consider these alternatives:
  • Clear specific data: You can delete individual lists, recipes, or inventory items from within the app
  • Remove shared access: Unshare your inventories or lists without deleting your account
  • Export your data: Download your information before deletion (available in app settings)
  • Temporary break: Simply log out and take a break - your data will remain safe

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)
  1. Open the GroceryPlus app
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Scroll to "Account Management"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately
Option 2: Email Request
  1. Send an email to the address below
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours
  5. You'll receive a confirmation when complete

Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion:

  • Immediate deletion: All personal data is removed from our active databases immediately
  • Backup retention: Automated backups may retain data for up to 30 days
  • Shared data: Content you shared with other users will remain in their accounts
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be retained for analytics
  • Legal obligations: We may retain certain data if required by law

Recovery Information

Account Recovery:
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered
  • You can create a new account with the same email after 30 days
  • Previous data will not be restored to new accounts
  • Shared access permissions cannot be restored
